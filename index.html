What We Automate

Four automations. Real, measurable results.

Every product is HIPAA-aware, up and running in days, and maintained by us — no IT team required on your end.

📧
Inbox Triage Bot
AI reads, classifies, and prioritizes every incoming email — then drafts ready-to-send replies for your staff to review. Urgent items surface immediately; routine requests handle themselves.
✓ Saves 5–8 hrs of inbox management per week
📱
Missed Call SMS Recovery
When your practice misses a call, a personalized SMS goes out within 60 seconds — automatically. Patients feel taken care of; you capture the appointment you almost lost.
✓ Recovering 2 new patients/mo pays for the entire service
📄
Digital Patient Intake
New patient forms are sent digitally before appointments. Data is captured and formatted automatically — no more clipboards, no more manual re-entry, no more transcription errors.
✓ Eliminates 3+ hours of staff data entry every week
🔔
Patient Recall & Follow-Up
Overdue patients are identified and automatically messaged with a personalized outreach sequence. No staff involvement. Just appointments that get booked — and revenue that comes back.
✓ 20 recalled patients/mo = $6,000 in recovered revenue
